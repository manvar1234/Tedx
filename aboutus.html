About Us

TEDxNISV is a student-led initiative curated and executed by learners at NISV. With the belief that powerful ideas can come from anyone, regardless of age, we aim to showcase voices that deserve to be heard.

Rooted in the global TED spirit of "Ideas Worth Spreading," our event serves as a platform for innovation, personal stories, and transformative visions that can inspire change and create impact. Each edition revolves around a unique theme that sparks curiosity and invites thoughtful discussion.

With KINETIKOS – Shift. Surge. Succeed. – we explore the essence of momentum, transformation, and progress. From science and psychology to art and activism, our stage welcomes stories that move.
